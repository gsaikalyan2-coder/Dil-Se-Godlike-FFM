Format

[edit]
  • Semi Finals: November 15 - 17, 2025
    • 18 teams.
      • 6 teams from Esports Qualifier.
      • 3 teams from YT Qualifer.
    • 18 matches, (6 each day).
    • Top 4 qualify for Grand Finals.
    • Rank 5 - 16 will advance to Wildcard.
  • Wildcard: November 18, 2025
    •  12 teams.
    •  6 matches.
    • Top 3 qualify for Grand Finals.
  • Grand Finals: November 19 - 20, 2025
    •  12 teams.
    •  12 matches, (6 each day).
