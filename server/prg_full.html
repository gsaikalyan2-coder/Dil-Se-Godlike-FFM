    Format

    [edit]
    • Qualifiers: Jan 28th - Feb 8th, 2026
    • Quarter Finals: February 9th, 2026
    • Semi Finals: February 10th - 11th, 2026
      •  24 teams.
        • 8 teams from Quarter Finals.
        • 16 invited teams.
      • Teams are divided into two groups of 12.
      •  12 matches, (6 each group).
      • Top 6 teams from each group Qualify for Grand Finals.
    • Grand Finals: February 12th - 17th, 2026
      •  12 teams.
      •  18 matches, (6 each day).
